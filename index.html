Frequently Asked Questions

I was born and raised in Homestead, FL. About an hour south of the city of Miami. I've lived there my whole life up until 2019 when I graduated highschool and moved to Tallahassee to pursue my degree.

I suppose for me the question has always been why not computer science. Junior year of highschool I decided to take a CS course and I've been hooked ever sense. Often times finding myself coding for hours on end just on personal projects. I figured if I'm always going to be coding anyway, I might as well make a carrer out of it.

I am on track to graduate May 2023 at Florida State University.

My strength doesn't come from a specific language or framework. But instead comes from my passion for programming. I might not be someone who is able to get an assignment and knows exactly what and how to do it. But I am the person who will sit there for as long as it takes to figure out how to solve the problem in question. No matter how much google searching, youtube watching, or documentation skimming it takes.

I am familiar with a lot of languages. Python, C++, Java, JavaScript, Swift, HTML, and CSS. However the languages I have the most experince with are Python and C++.

Yes! I actually am familiar with a quite a few frameworks and libraries. My favorite libraries to work with are mostly in Python. These include Selinium, BS4, TensorFlow, and PyGame.
